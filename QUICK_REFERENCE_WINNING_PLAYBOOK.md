## THE 3-MINUTE WINNING STRUCTURE

### **OPENING (0:00-0:30) - THE HOOK**
**Goal**: Create immediate emotional investment

**Winner Techniques**:
- üî• **Kluben 2021**: "People used to shop TOGETHER, now they shop ALONE"

‚ùå **NEVER DO**: Generic greeting, feature announcement, delayed hook

---

### **PROBLEM (0:30-1:00) - THE REFRAME**
**Goal**: Show you understand the ROOT CAUSE others miss

**Winner Techniques**:
- üî• **Chile**: "The real issue isn't hair falling out. It's men falling off the routine."
- üî• **Kluben**: Problem isn't e-commerce, it's LONELINESS
- üî• **All Beauty**: Problem isn't makeup products, it's APPLICATION TOOLS

**Formula**: Surface Problem ‚Üí Real Problem (behavioral/emotional)

‚ùå **NEVER DO**: List features, focus only on functional gaps

---

### **SOLUTION (1:00-2:00) - THE ECOSYSTEM**
**Goal**: Show complete system through character using it

**Winner Techniques**:
- Show character journey (Hannah & Maria, Alex, etc.)
- Walk through ecosystem (Device + App + Community + Data)
- Demonstrate L'Oreal asset integration (ModiFace, Perso)
- Prove with data/user validation

**Formula**: Character USES solution ‚Üí Transformation visible ‚Üí Tech enables behavior

‚ùå **NEVER DO**: Feature dump, jargon overload, no character

---

### **BUSINESS (2:00-2:30) - THE MODEL**
**Goal**: Show clear path to recurring revenue

**Winner Models**:
- **Razor-Blade**: Device + refills (Chile, Rocket Paper Sisters)
- **Conversion**: Free integration, revenue through sales increase (Kluben)
- **Platform**: Free for L'Oreal, fee for others (Kluben expansion)

**Formula**: One-time sale (acquisition) + Recurring revenue (LTV) = Clear profitability

‚ùå **NEVER DO**: "Indirect benefits," vague monetization, no pricing

---

### **CLOSING (2:30-3:00) - THE HOPE**
**Goal**: Deliver unforgettable line that gives hope + action

**Winner Examples**:
- üî• **Chile**: "Your hair isn't gone. It's just waiting for instructions. So let's grow."
- üî• **Kluben**: "We bring back the FUN experience"
- üî• **All Beauty**: "When a person cannot express beauty freely...it means that was WRONG"

**Formula**: Hope + Empowerment + Collective Action ("Let's")

‚ùå **NEVER DO**: "Thank you," feature summary, no emotional payoff

## THE BUSINESS MODEL PLAYBOOK

### MODEL 1: RAZOR-BLADE (Highest Winner Rate)
**Structure**:
- Device/Kit: $100-400 (customer acquisition)
- Refills: $20-50/month (high-margin recurring)
- Accessories: $10-30 every 90 days (additional revenue)

**Examples**:
- Chile: Device + serum refills + needle heads
- Rocket Paper Sisters: Pod + refill cartridges
- Mausoleu: Device + test strips + cartridge refills

**Margins**:
- Device: Low margin (10-20%)
- Refills: High margin (60-80%)
- LTV: 2-5 years of recurring purchases

---

### MODEL 2: CONVERSION IMPROVEMENT (2nd Highest)
**Structure**:
- Free integration (no separate product pricing)
- Revenue through increased e-commerce conversion
- ROI through sales lift

**Example**:
- Kluben: "ModiFace doubles conversion rates, Kluben adds social = even higher conversion"

**Proof Needed**:
- Baseline conversion rate
- Expected lift percentage
- Revenue impact calculation

---

### MODEL 3: PLATFORM PLAY (Emerging)
**Structure**:
- Free for L'Oreal brands (customer acquisition)
- Fee for non-L'Oreal brands (monetization)
- Data licensing (future revenue)

**Example**:
- Kluben: Free for L'Oreal products, fee for other brands on platform

**Requirements**:
- Network effects to drive adoption
- Clear value prop for both sides
- Data moat for defensibility
