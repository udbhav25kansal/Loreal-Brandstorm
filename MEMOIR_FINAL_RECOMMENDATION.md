## THE VERDICT: VERSION 5 IS THE WINNER

**Version 5** is the most complete and defensible model because:

### 1. Simplicity Without Sacrifice
- Reduced to essentials: 3 steps, one price, clear guarantee
- But retains depth for every judge question
- The "2-5 minutes" input requirement is realistic and low-friction

### 2. Addresses the Core Tension
The fundamental problem from the transcript was: "How do you ensure someone likes a scent they've never smelled?"

V5 solves this elegantly:
- **"Avoidance" input** = prevents major mismatches
- **Memory Portrait preview** = sets expectations before creation
- **60-day adjustment guarantee** = safety net if it doesn't resonate
- **One adjustment, not unlimited** = financially sustainable

### 3. AI is Credible
V5 explains the technology in a way that's:
- Grounded in existing L'Oreal capabilities
- Specific about what AI does vs. what perfumer does
- Not over-promised (AI assists, doesn't replace artisans)

### 4. Pricing is Justified
The comparison table showing MEMOIR at $299 vs. Le Labo at $230 vs. Guerlain bespoke at $3000+ makes the value proposition crystal clear.

### 5. Slides Are Designed to Win
V5 includes actual slide content with design notes, not just bullet points. The emotional opening, simple middle, and impact-focused close follow winning pitch structure.

### 6. All Loopholes Closed
Every concern from the transcript has a specific answer:

| Transcript Concern | V5 Solution |
|--------------------|-------------|
| "Can't describe scents" | Photo + text + single emotion question |
| "What if they don't like it?" | Preview + 60-day adjustment guarantee |
| "Tech for tech's sake?" | AI does the impossible task; humans do the artistry |
| "In-store vs. digital?" | Digital-first, globally accessible |
| "Is it scalable?" | Batch perfumer model detailed |
| "Different from competitors?" | Photo-to-fragrance is unique positioning |
| "Abstract memories?" | Text option explicitly supported |

## WHAT V5 DOES THAT OTHERS DON'T

1. **Optional "avoidance" input** - Elegant solution to prevent disasters without requiring positive scent articulation

2. **Memory Portrait preview** - Gives customer a moment of anticipation AND a chance to course-correct

3. **60-day guarantee with one adjustment limit** - Generous enough to build trust, limited enough to be sustainable

4. **3-photo option** - More data = better results, but not required

5. **Written memory option** - Expands market to pre-smartphone memories, deceased loved ones, abstract feelings

6. **Unit economics included** - Shows judges you've thought about business viability

7. **Phase-based scaling plan** - Demonstrates realistic growth path

8. **Design notes on slides** - Shows understanding of presentation craft
