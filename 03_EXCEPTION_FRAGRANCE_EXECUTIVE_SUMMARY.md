### 03 | AND A VERY TANGIBLE ONE: A ONE-OF-A-KIND JUICE

**The juice is the entry point to the category**

**Key Statistics:**
- **30%** of conversation on social media are about scent profiles and notes
- **29%** of virality is based on scent profiles
- **Top level technique** is a must-have

---

**Expert Consumers Look For:**
- Signature scents
- Unusual note combination
- Precious ingredients
- Technical performance
- **Olfactive accidents**

**Newcomer Consumers Look For:**
- An **"It-bag" scent**: luxury, statutory but still safe choice

---

**Perfumers' Perspective on Exception Juice:**

**What Makes a Killer Juice?**

‚úÖ **All 12 perfumes** convey an **immediate message**: simple and qualitative olfactory message
- Can even be "raw": close to an accord (Baccarat Rouge and Santal 33)

‚úÖ **Strong "partis pris"** - often an ingredient played in **overdose** that creates an **olfactive incident**

‚úÖ **Deconstruction of classic pyramid** (top-heart-bottom)
- Not always immediate crush (vs prestige)
- Time must be given to **skin revelation**
- Crush may only appear at heart or/and bottom

‚úÖ **Follow one direction** (memory, ingredient, emotions...)
- Keep this **red thread** along whole process
- **Few mods, simple story**, true to artistic pulse

---

**Different Segments Follow Different Creative Processes:**

**Pure Player Brands:**
- Can be more **polarizing, less consensual**
- Pure, extreme, radical
- Even non-identifiable to something already existing

**Couture Brands:**
- More **multi-layered and complex**
- Powerful notes, premium scents
- Less radical

---

**MAJOR INSIGHT:**

> Just like ready-to-wear is designed to meet most consumers' taste and expectations, prestige is designed to trigger immediate crush and massive approval. On the other hand, **exception is to fragrance what couture is to fashion**: the consumer comes here to be offered a **vision, result of a creative pulse**.
