Key Insights Summary

Consumer Needs:

  • Sensorial Stimulation: Perfume as physical addictive and pleasurable experience
  • Personal Assertion: Emotional positive experience that makes people reconnect with themselves
  • Mindful Wellness: Mindful passport and enabler that helps people belong, socialize, and influence
  • Social Impact: Cultural booster that unlocks private fantasies, stimulates personal growth, enriches identity

Market Dynamics:

US & Europe:

  • Daily usage increasing across all generations
  • Western Europe remains 1st WW market in usage penetration (78% of selective women use perfume daily)
  • US has made progress (58.7% Gen2 usage in 2021)
  • Fragrance use mainly linked to MINDFUL SELFCARE

China:

  • Daily use of perfume has become a habit for more than half (53%) of selective Chinese women
  • Usage increase between 2017 and 2021 especially strong among millennials (+12% Millenials vs 10% Gen Z)
  • New way of expressing themselves: marker of fashion savvy and edginess, personal and social differentiation
  • Exception is driving the market: 34% of selective Chinese perfume users have used Exception level perfume in past 6 months (vs 10% Europe / 13% US)

Generational Insights:

Gen Z (15-25):

  • Variety and opportunity
  • Fragrant pleasure beyond just smelling
  • Personalized discovery
  • Demand for smart transparency

Millennials (26-39):

  • Perfume with tangible one-off appeal
  • Freshness & confidence

Gen X (40-54):

  • Pampering without bling
  • Social impact, fashion power

Key Preferences:

  • Femininity Boost (US): Freshness + Sexy Confidence
  • Mindful Selfcare (Global): Smell Fresh, Feel Good
  • Me-Boost (China): Social Elevation + Personal Elevation
  • Me-Ritual (Global): Self-pampering + Wellbeing

Category Trends:

Access:

  • Air Revitalization, Strong identity, Home rituals, Forbidden dreams, Fashionable image

Enhanced Experience:

  • Perfume key needs: Home rituals, Smell fresh, Sharp identitiy, Forbidden dreams, Fashionable image

Exception as Absolute Reference:

  • Online chat about perfumes reaching unprecedented levels
  • Sophisticated narratives expected to provide dreams
  • Vitality is necessary but not sufficient success booster
  • Point of Sale experience keeps lagging although it is a key for perfume

  1. Exception is the Eldorado of the fragrance category with +27% vs LY growth
