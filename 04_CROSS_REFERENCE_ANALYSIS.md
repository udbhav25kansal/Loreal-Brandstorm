## RISK FACTORS & MITIGATION

### Risk #1: Expert Abandonment
**Threat:** "Once newcomers adopt, experts leave" (Exception Study)

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Limited editions maintaining confidentiality
- Tiered collections (exception within exception)
- Expert-only experiences/services
- Co-creation with expert communities
- Maintain olfactive avant-garde regardless of commercial success

---

### Risk #2: Dupe Proliferation
**Threat:** Success attracts dupes; dupes growing +49% YoY

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Layer undupable elements (tech + proprietary ingredients + ecosystem)
- Educate on safety/ethics (Recommendation #6 from Dupe Study)
- Emphasize drydown complexity dupes cannot match
- Build refill infrastructure creating switching costs
- Merge efforts between brands for industry-wide standards

---

### Risk #3: Shooting Star Virality
**Threat:** Fast viral success without foundation = Gris Dior (198M views, ‚Ç¨0.1M sales)

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Prioritize exceptional juice before viral activation
- Accept longer development time (Libre: 7 years)
- Build for endurance, not just moment
- Strategic seeding with experts first, then virality
- Measure success over 3-5 years, not 3-5 months

---

### Risk #4: Sustainability-Desirability Trade-off
**Threat:** Sustainable solutions perceived as less luxurious

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Frame refillability as "made to last" luxury (already doing this)
- Premium refill ritual design (fountain experience)
- Celebrity/ambassador demonstration (Emma Watson, Dua Lipa, Austin Butler)
- Make refills "the new normal" (World Refill Day campaign)
- Showcase Osmobloom‚Ñ¢ as sustainable AND superior

---

### Risk #5: Tech Overwhelm vs. Artisanal Craft
**Threat:** Too much tech alienates experts seeking human artistry

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Technology enhances, never replaces, perfumer vision
- Keep tech "invisible" in product, visible in experience
- Frame AI as tool for personalization, not creation
- Maintain "Art & Science" balance (Mission core principle)
- Lead with perfumer story, support with tech capability
